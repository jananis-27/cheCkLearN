Career Building Platform

This is a simple career building platform designed to help students understand what skills and domains they should learn for their future. It provides a clear checklist of programming language topics and software domains so that students can plan their learning step by step. By using this platform, students can explore different career paths, track what they have learned, and build a strong foundation for their career with clarity and confidence.
